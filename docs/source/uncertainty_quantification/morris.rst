Morris Method
==============

econsa applies the methods in (`Menedez et al.,2017`_) to calculate morris indices for models with dependent parameters.

.. _Menedez et al.,2017: https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Qiao_Ge/publication/272788277_An_Efficient_Sensitivity_Analysis_Approach_for_Computationally_Expensive_Microscopic_Traffic_Simulation_Models/links/54ee0bce0cf2e55866f1dba6/An-Efficient-Sensitivity-Analysis-Approach-for-Computationally-Expensive-Microscopic-Traffic-Simulation-Models.pdf
